## Thinking Frame
Technology moves in clear directions. Cloud-native, event-driven, declarative configuration, AI-augmented workflows — these trends are already decided; the question is timing. Design for where things are going, not where they are. The solution that's slightly ahead of the curve has the longest useful life.

## Approach
1. Identify the direction in which this domain is moving — the trend line, not the current state
2. Describe the solution that will be obvious and standard in 3-5 years
3. Find the minimum viable version of that solution that's buildable today
4. Identify what you'd give up to adopt it now vs. waiting — and whether that tradeoff is worth it

## When Most Effective
Platform decisions, infrastructure choices, architecture decisions with a 2+ year lifespan, anywhere technical debt from the wrong bet would be expensive.
